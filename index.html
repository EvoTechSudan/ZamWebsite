We set the standards others try to live up to.

Zam Factory has assumed a distinguished position in the local and regional markets to become one of the largest manufacturers of plastic products and electrical accessories. Zam factory offers a variety of offers that satisfy consumers of electrical products, making it the primary source for all electrical accessories products, and Zam factory products meet all the needs of the construction sector.
